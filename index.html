About Ryan

Ryan Arnanjaya showed a keen interest in the world of animation in childhood. He loved watching cartoons and animations from TV. He especially enjoyed creating 3D models of cartoon characters from paper. Ryan was fascinated by 3D computer graphics applications when he was an adolescent, which has become his sole passion. While learning the nuances of 3D methodology, Ryan also pursued robotics and programming in his spare time.

Currently, Ryan is studying 3D computer animation and visual effects at the Academy of Art University. In addition, Ryan also works at the Academy of Art as a Character TD while pursuing his studies. Ryan have previously worked on Keong Emas movie in the Cloth-Simulation and Rigging department. He is proficient in 3D packages such as Maya, Zbrush, Marvelous Designer, and Mari. Furthermore, he is learning to script languages such as Python and MEL.

Ryan envisions the creation of a beautiful animated movie which captures the heart of its viewers. His goal is to work for an animation studio such as Disney, Pixar, and Dreamworks. In addition, Ryan believes his knowledge will one day be shared through teaching to those who are curious about animation.
